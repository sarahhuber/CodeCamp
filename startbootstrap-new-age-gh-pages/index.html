Half of Iowa State's Undergraduates are in STEM fields, yet the college of engineering is only 15% women.




Our mission is to connect women in engineering at Iowa State past and present to build a community that mentors and empowers one another.

With a mentor, two is better than one

This site can be used to connect you with other women in your field. Please fill out our mentor form and we will pair you with a woman in your field who resonates with your background. This effort would not be possible without volunteers to mentor Iowa State Students. We invite you, as a woman in the workplace, professor, or upperclasswoman to sign up and become a mentor.
